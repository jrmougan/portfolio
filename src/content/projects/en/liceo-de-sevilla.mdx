## About the project

**Liceo de Sevilla** is the website for a music association based in Seville, Spain. The project started as a volunteering collaboration to give the association a digital presence and help manage their activities.

## Tech stack

- **Backend**: Laravel (PHP), with an admin panel for content management
- **Database**: MySQL
- **Frontend**: Blade templates + TailwindCSS
- **Deployment**: Shared hosting, in production

## My role

I participated as a volunteer, collaborating on:

- Design and implementation of public-facing views
- Integration with the admin panel
- Mobile device optimization

## Status

The project is **in production** and live at [liceodesevilla.com](https://liceodesevilla.com).
